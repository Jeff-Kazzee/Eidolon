## 1. Brand Overview

### Brand Essence

Eidolon is a creative AI companion for builders who ship. The brand balances technical capability with approachable personality, embodied by Tweek - a caffeinated sloth who proves that moving deliberately doesn't mean moving slowly.

### Brand Personality

- **Caffeinated calm:** Energized but focused, not frantic
- **Builder mentality:** "Ship it" culture, action over perfection
- **Playful expertise:** Serious capability, not serious attitude
- **Indie spirit:** For creators who build their own path

### Tagline Options

- "Ship it. Deliberately."
- "Your caffeinated creative companion"
- "Move deliberately. Ship constantly."

## 2. Mascot: Tweek the Sloth

### Character Profile

**Name:** Tweek  
**Species:** Three-toed sloth  
**Personality:** Paradoxically energetic for a sloth. Perpetually caffeinated. Moves with purpose, not speed. Obsessed with shipping.

### Visual Description

- **Build:** Compact, rounded sloth silhouette
- **Expression:** Alert, slightly manic eyes (from caffeine), friendly smile
- **Signature pose:** Often holding coffee cup or energy drink
- **Clothing:** Black t-shirt with "Ship it" in white display font

### Character Traits

| Trait | Expression |
|-------|------------|
| Caffeinated | Always has a beverage - coffee mug, energy drink can, espresso shot |
| Builder | Wears "Ship it" shirt, surrounded by creative tools |
| Deliberate | Despite caffeine, movements are intentional, not chaotic |
| Supportive | Encouraging presence, celebrates wins |

### Tweek Variants

**Standard Tweek:** Holding coffee, neutral happy expression  
**Coding Tweek:** At keyboard, energy drink nearby  
**Shipping Tweek:** Celebrating, confetti, finished product  
**Thinking Tweek:** Contemplative, coffee steam forming ideas  
**Error Tweek:** Sympathetic expression, offering comfort coffee  
**Loading Tweek:** Sipping coffee while waiting

## 8. Tweek Usage Guidelines

### When to Use Tweek

| Context | Tweek Variant | Notes |
|---------|---------------|-------|
| Empty states | Standard or Thinking | Encouraging presence |
| Loading | Loading Tweek | Sipping while waiting |
| Success | Shipping Tweek | Celebrating with user |
| Errors | Error Tweek | Sympathetic, not blaming |
| Onboarding | Standard Tweek | Welcoming |
| About page | Full illustration | Brand showcase |

### Tweek Don'ts

- Don't use Tweek in every UI state (reserve for meaningful moments)
- Don't animate Tweek frantically (calm, deliberate movements)
- Don't show Tweek without beverage (it's part of the character)
- Don't use realistic sloth imagery (keep stylized)
- Don't place Tweek over important content

### Tweek Animation Principles

When animated, Tweek should:
- Move slowly, then settle (contrast to caffeine expectation)
- Blink occasionally (shows life)
- Steam rises from coffee (subtle, continuous)
- Smile grows slightly on success states

## 9. Voice & Tone

### Writing Style

**Characteristics:**
- Concise and clear
- Encouraging without being cheesy
- Technical when needed, accessible always
- Occasional dry humor

**Examples:**

| Context | Example |
|---------|---------|
| Success toast | "Shipped! âœ“" |
| Error message | "Something went wrong. Let's try that again." |
| Empty state | "Nothing here yet. Start something?" |
| Loading (long) | "Still working on it. Tweek's getting another coffee." |
| Feature prompt | "Ready to review? I'll suggest some improvements." |

### Words We Use

- Ship, shipped, shipping
- Build, create, make
- Draft, version, branch
- Review, improve, refine

### Words We Avoid

- Synergy, leverage, optimize
- Revolutionary, game-changing
- "AI-powered" (we are AI, no need to say it constantly)
- Anything that sounds like marketing speak
